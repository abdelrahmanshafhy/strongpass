Hard to hack:


Hackers always seek to hack and steal accounts for different purposes.

In order for the hacker to hack your account through the e-mail, but some people think that
giving the e-mail to anyone will not harm, but this person may be a hacker and through
his knowledge of the e-mail he can guess your password.

But some ask how hackers can guess the password?

The answer, simply, is that by knowing your date of birth, your name, your address,

So you should create a password that is not linked to anything in your life or something

And now you definitely need tips to avoid the problem of hacking and theft of accounts, data and personal
information:

  • Try to choose a strong password, for example, that contains uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers and
    symbols.

  • Make sure the person you are sending the email to has personal information.

  • Try to avoid questionable sites and unknown shopping sites and not to enter account information or
    banking information (credit card information).

  • Avoid sites that say you won a phone or a car because they are mostly hacker pages to let you enter your
    data to steal it.

    When you enter the websites of movies and series, sometimes pages are opened that require you to
    enter your data for different reasons, so try to avoid these things and do not fall into the hands of the
    hacker.

  • Sometimes hackers resort to the idea of hacking through emails.
    Because by opening the message, you are allowing and giving permission to the hacker to be able to
    successfully complete the hack.

    Because the message is just a way to reach you.

    So always check the messages before opening them and also check the source of the message.

  • Beware of Wi-Fi networks in the streets and public places, because sometimes they are under
    the control of an intruder.

    When you connect to these networks, you allow the hacker to take control of your phone and gain access
    to your personal information.

    And always make sure to download security and protection software and activate the firewall.

  • But you should also pay attention when you enter websites, pay attention to the icons next to the domain
    name.
    As you can see in the following table, these codes are safe, unsafe, and dangerous.

    Icon Category Browser rating
    safe Connection is secure Your information (for example, passwords or credit card numbers) is
    private when it is sent to this site.
    unsafe Your connection to this site is not secure You should not enter any sensitive information on
    this site (for example, passwords or credit cards), because it could be stolen by attackers.
    dangerous Your connection to this site is not secure You should not enter any sensitive information on
    this site (for example, passwords or credit cards), because it could be stolen by attackers.

    So be aware of websites before entering your information such as email, password and credit card
    information.

And now you are definitely wondering how I can act if my device or my account has been hacked.

  • First, keep your connection to the Internet as short as possible so that the hacker cannot access the
    rest of the information.

  • Secondly, download protection programs and encryption programs.

  • Third, contact the protection companies and people who specialize in this field and have high knowledge
    of these matters.

  • Fourth, visit the nearest phone repair shop because they have enough experience in dealing with these
    matters.

  • Fifthly, watch videos to help you solve this problem.

    Do a Google search for solutions to this problem and focus on the experiences of the people behind this
    topic.

  • Finally, after restoring your data, format your device or perform a factory reset.

    And if you do not know how to do a hard reset or factory reset, go to the nearest electronics store and
    there you will find someone who can do that for you.

    There are also programs dedicated to these matters, which is a somewhat easier way.
